In the age of more, businesses and consumers are faced with innumerable choices. This leads to sub-optimal decisions being made, and increasingly dissatisfied consumers. Crayon Data aims to solve this problem by simplifying the world’s choices. Currently, Crayon’s products and solutions are designed for the hospitality, retail, banking and telecom verticals.

We are a well funded, fast growing technology start-up located in Singapore, with our development centre in Chennai. We have sales offices in the UK, US and Middle East. Our founders are successful entrepreneurs, who have been in the business of making data work for businesses and consumers.

We are looking to build a team of inspired, self-driven, analytical, imaginative, flexible and focused individuals; to bring great energy into a new enterprise with their experience, skills and winning attitude. If you are looking to join a team of eclectic people who relish the challenge of riding the big data wave, then we look forward to speaking with you.

Specialties
