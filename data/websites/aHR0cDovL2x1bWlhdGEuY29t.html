Selected Case Studies:

 

Population Analytics
Large Payer Organization
Lumiata completed a Gaps in Diagnosis Analysis for a major regional health plan on a dataset of 100,000 Medicare Advantage lives resulting in predicted $100MM+ increase in reimbursement, with sensitivities and specificities in the high 70′s and 80′s across most major diseases.

 

Point of Care
Nurse Call Center
Lumiata completed pilots with both a nurse call center and an urgent care over hundreds of patient encounters showing an improvement of 30%-40% in triage time.

 

Digital & Tele-Health
Village Health Workers
Lumiata completed a pilot with village health care workers in India, with over 3,000 patient encounters, helping increase the access to quality medical care by combining Lumiata’s real-time analytics with SMS.

Chief Executive Officer

As the Founder and CEO of Lumiata, Ash Damle drives the company’s mission to transform and advance the world’s medical knowledge into practical insights. Lumiata has developed a first-of-kind medical graph based on the most current scientific research and clinical practice.
