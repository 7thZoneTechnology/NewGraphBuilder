At Zerofootprint, we inspire and incent behavioral change that is good for people, good for our communities and good for the planet.

Our goal is to enhance the GOOD in communities. To get there we are partnering with local governments, utilities, organizations, corporations, both public and private to reach individuals. What links our partners at the outset is an imperative to enable their employees/citizens/customers to lead more healthy and fulfilled lifestyles.

We are a socially responsible enterprise whose mission is to apply technology, program design and risk management to improve the health of our populations, reduce our consumption of fossil fuels and facilitate the wide scale reduction of our environmental footprint. While our impacts are diverse and interconnected, we specialize in developing programs that motivate people towards a path of positive change and build resilience in each of these three core areas:
