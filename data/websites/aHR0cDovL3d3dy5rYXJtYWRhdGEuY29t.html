karmadata in a nutshell

karmadata is a collection of the world's healthcare data, all standardized, linked, and accessible in one place. We want you to ask and answer healthcare questions that matter, to you, to the world. The data is standardized into common healthcare entities (Physicians, Clinical Trial Data, Clinical Investigators, Hospitals, Pharmacies, Drugs, Diseases, Geography, etc.), enabling users to visualize and (simply) query the world's healthcare data.

Search and Browse

We offer everything from the basic keyword search (one text box only please!) to the complex filtering by dates, geography, disease, drug, company, person, etc..

Visualize and Analyze

This is where your talent to identify healtcare problems and solutions can shine. Join multiple sources together into an amazing mashup. The best data cards are recognized every week.

Share and Follow

You can follow other users, or any one of our entities (companies, drugs, people, etc.) and receive a real time feed of their activities on the site or in the data we mine.

Standardized, Linked, Share or Keep Private

How is karmadata different?

You don't need a corporate subscription to access top notch healthcare data. We offer private sandboxes for corporations that need privacy. We treat each open data source as a global treasure trove waiting to be mined. We treat HIPAA facing data such as insurance claims and provider records with the tightest of security. We find the best possible source for a particular information need, and using our API bring that source directly to you.

Government and Regulatory Agencies

Open Data

The World Health Organization, Medicare, Medicaid, HealthData.gov, FDA, NIH, CDC, and countless other agencies provide awesome starting points for understanding the global healthcare market. Sadly these source records need a LOT of work before they can be analyzed. First the data is HUGE. Multiple terrabytes. Second each data source is messy and unstandardized. Finally none of these great healthcare data sources are linked to each other. We provide a scalable platform where they are all in a standard format and LINKED together. A Data Scientist's dream.

Healthcare Providers, Insurance Plans and IT Systems

Private Data

Everyone reading this has been to a doctors office, or picked up a prescription at their local pharmacy. Some of you may even have an electronic medical record. Hopefully most of your physicians do. This is the really BIG healthcare data: interactions between consumers of healthcare (you) and the providers of healthcare (doctors, pharmacists, etc.). There is tremendous value in mining this data, and creating Apps to empower consumers, and drive down the costs of healthcare. We are an active participant in this market. We envision a brave new world where consumers have enough information to make informed decisions and engage in active conversations with their provider.

karmadata API

karmadata provides a standard RESTful API to query the world's healthcare data, and to receive results in JSON, XML, Excel, Google DataTable formats (more formats being added). Developers with an API key can build their own apps mashing our Open data sources against their own Private Data.
