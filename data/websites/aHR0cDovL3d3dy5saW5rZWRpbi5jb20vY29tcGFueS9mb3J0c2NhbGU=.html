The challenging world of enterprise security demands challenging solutions.

Fortscale is an innovator in User Behavior Analytics for Security. Our goal is to enable enterprise security analysts to easily generate insights about users, by enriching existing log repositories such as SIEM.

With these analytics, Fortscale makes it easier to discover malicious, compromised or risky users and to quickly investigate them. The Fortscale solution integrates a proven big data platform with advanced machine learning algorithms and is successfully installed in Fortune 1000 companies.

The company is backed by Intel Capital and Blumberg Capital, with headquarters in San Francisco and R&D in Tel Aviv, Israel.

Specialties

Cyber Security, Intelligence, Big Data, Analytics, User Intelligence, User Behavior Analyitcs, Machine Learning
