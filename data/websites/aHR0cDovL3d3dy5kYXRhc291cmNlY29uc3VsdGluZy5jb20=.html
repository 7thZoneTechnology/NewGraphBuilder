Datasource Consulting is a premier leader in Enterprise Data Management (EDM) and Business Intelligence (BI). We specialize in delivering Lean, innovative, end-to-end solutions that leverage and express the full value of data.

We employ the best and brightest experts in the industry with a focus on areas such as data integration, data architecture, data virtualization, data modeling, data quality, data governance, reporting & analytics, and BI program management. Datasource Consulting not only remains close to the latest technology and industry trends, but we also help move the needle with thought leadership in areas such as BI in the Cloud, Agile BI, and Big Data.

Empowered with a proven methodology that embraces Agile and Lean principles, Datasource Consulting knows how to make your project and program successful. Please explore our site to learn how.
