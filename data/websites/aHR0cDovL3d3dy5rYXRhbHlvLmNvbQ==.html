Katalyo is a work and collaboration solution that will innovate the way people work and collaborate and take productivity to the next level.


The power of simplicity

The foundation of Katalyo relies on the basic principle of work: The Input->Work->Output concept. It can not get more simple than that but the thing is it works. This is how things get done in the real world and to be truly efficient the supporting software must embrace this concept. Katalyo does.

Visualize your work

Picture is worth a thousand words and with Katalyo all your work becomes visual. Your job is to work and Katalyo process mining engine will derive context, relationships and generate visual workflows that no wording can match.


Work and collaborate in your own way

With Katalyo people take matters into their own hands. Katalyo provides the tools that empower people to adapt the way they work on a particular task and aligned it with the needed context.

Re-use your work

The ability to re-use previous work is essential and basicaly enables creation and preservation of knowledge. Katalyo provides a set of tools that will break down you work into reusable components that are used as a template the next time same or similar type of work has to be completed.
