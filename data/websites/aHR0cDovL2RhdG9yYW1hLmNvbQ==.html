The Marketer’s Data Hub: Get in Control, Fast.

Datorama brings a new approach to marketing analytics: The Marketer’s Data Hub. Now you can have a clear view of, and control over, all of your marketing activities and media channels’ performance. Make your next move quickly and with confidence: Our end-to-end marketing intelligence platform integrates, standardizes and visualizes all of your campaigns and activities so you can easily analyze and refine them within minutes.

Get your whole team to move quickly and with confidence.

CMOs

  • Increase data transparency and manage your team with easily digestible data insights.
  • Make real-time adjustments in media spend, engagements and resource allocation.
  • Monitor top-line KPIs based on every marketing channel available.

Analysts

  • Use existing APIs or easily create a custom API-like connection in minutes to map data.
  • Plug and play any marketing data across campaigns or brands to make powerful insights in real-time.
  • Provide and control universal access to reporting and insights across your entire team and your client base.
  • Visualize and export data in ready-to-present formats.

IT

  • Fast data onboarding: Get up and running in minutes… literally.
  • Platform scalability – adjust Datorama to fit your needs as your business grows in complexity and size.
  • Security: The latest in multilevel authentication for access and a user role system that allows for data to be shared only with those who were meant to see it.
