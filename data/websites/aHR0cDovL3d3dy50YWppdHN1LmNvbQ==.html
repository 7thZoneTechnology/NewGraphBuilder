ABOUT

Tajitsu was founded in 2011 with a mission to help marketing teams turn data into results. The technology is based on decades of AI research at Chalmers University and has been used commercially for over 10 years.

In September 2011, Tajitsu Industries was voted to be the second most promising start-up in the commercial gaming market by EIG LaunchPad 2011 (the global i-gaming industry’s main conference and forum).

Tajitsu is working with clients around the globe, and has offices in Sweden, Africa and Asia.
