What is a fundability score?

We created a scoring system based upon the characteristics of successful entrepreneurs, so that you can evaluate your traits from an investor's point of view.

A fundability score can help you:

Network

Your connections with successful entrepreneurs, mentors, experts and investors within your particular industry.
