Motto

Über mich
Platfora makes knowing possible. Most companies that think they’re using big data aren’t. Without realizing it, they’re only seeing a fraction of what they have. Platfora is a self-service, iterative, and fast platform that makes it possible to leverage all of your organization’s data, so you can unlock insights, find new opportunities, make more meaningful decisions, and produce better outcomes. Platfora’s intuitive visual interface encourages you to ask new questions about your data and makes it easy to follow hunches, test theories, and keep refining your search until you find exactly what you are looking for—all with no coding required. 

The only complete, end-to-end analytics platform built natively on Hadoop, Platfora Big Data Analytics is creating data-driven competitive advantages in the areas of security, marketing, finance, operations and the Internet of Things for leading organizations such as Citi, Sears, AutoTrader, Disney, Edmunds.com, Opower, Riot Games, Vivint and TUI Travel.
