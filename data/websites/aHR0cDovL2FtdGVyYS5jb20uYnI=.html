About Amtera

Back to top

Amtera is an Information Technology company that aims to provide intelligent systems based on Semantic Technologies and Big Data. Our core expertise area is search and semantic processing of information for Big Data scenarios.

We work to address common demands within knowledge-centric organizations such as intelligent knowledge management, intelligent intranets, data integration, customer relations automation, among others.

We provide highly specialized products and services (consulting and training) in Semantic Technologies and Big Data.

Esprit© Search Platform

Esprit is a simple-to-use knowledge management tool based on semantic search, focused on corporate and government markets, where users can quickly and efficiently share and find information (in 2-3 steps).

Read more
Sentient© Web Marketing

Sentient is a Web platform which supports organizations in their Web marketing campaigns. Sentient allows the automated sentiment analysis of consumer opinions related to an organization or its products. However, Sentient goes beyond sentiment analysis, allowing its users to have the consumer profile of its audience, to measure and understand the impact of each campaign and to organize the communication with its customers.
