Service
Popcloud
Access Content Anywhere, Anytime, Any device


Popcloud is a synchronized file saving and sharing service based
on a cloud computing platform. Whenever a new file is generated,
all devices with Popcloud installed are synchronized to give access to the file.
Popcloud allows you to share and use images, music, videos and documents saved
in your Popcloud personal storage, anywhere and at any time.
Share a Popcloud folder with a friend, family member or co-worker, and they can directly
access the folder on their own PC. You can also send links to specific files uploaded to Popcloud!

Features of Popcloud
Upload
Upload your valuable documents, images and videos to Popcloud, and you can access your uploads with any media device, including multiple PCs, tablets and mobile devices.
Sharing
Share documents for collaboration using Popcloud. Any change in a shared file is automatically saved to prevent problems.
Popsync
Having a problem with your network? Contents saved on Popsync are accessible with any device, at any place and at any time, even when there is a problem in your network. Edited files are automatically synchronized on all devices once the network is recovered.
Links
Documents uploaded to Popcloud are easily shared with your co-workers, family and friends. You donât have to worry about file sizes, or compress files to send them via e-mail or messenger anymore!
Camera uploads
Choose the option of auto uploading your mobile images and videos. The Camera Upload function will automatically upload contents in media devices like mobile phones or tablets to Popcloud when the devices are connected to PCs.
