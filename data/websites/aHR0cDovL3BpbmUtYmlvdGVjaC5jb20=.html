Big Data Analysis Application

Big Data Analysis opens up new opportunities for the world of Agrotech and Biotech. Our company delivers these solutions to help industries face the future.

  • Virology

    Computational biological methods are changing. Today there are unprecedented opportunities for better diagnostics, therapeutics and vaccines within this discipline. Our intent is to develop and deploy a computational platform for big data. A key element of this development is the union of advanced experimental data with analysis and integration algorithms that are tuned to the data specificity.

  • Bioinformatics

    The analytical approach we have developed provides an opportunity to examine and quantify evolutionary dynamics at nucleotide resolution on a genome-wide scale, and to integrate evolutionary information with structural and physiological data.

  • Personalized Medicine

    Modern advances in Personalized Medicine rely on technology that confirms a patient's fundamental biology, DNA, RNA, or protein, which ultimately leads to confirming disease. For example, personalized medicine techniques such as genome sequencing can reveal mutations in the DNA code that lead to diseases ranging from cystic fibrosis to cancer.

  • Discovery Platform

    Utilizing bioinformatics analysis in our approach to Big Data, we can assist in drug and disease discovery in collaboration with medical and scientific research teams
