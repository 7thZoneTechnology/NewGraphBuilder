METASTARTUP: (N.), A STARTUP THAT WORKS TO IMPROVE THE PROCESS OF LAUNCHING STARTUPS

Trendify brings objective, data-based analysis to the startup ecosystem. Our software analyzes hand-validated data to both determine the exit likelihood of private companies and explain why they might succeed or fail by programmatically highlighting their strengths and weaknesses. Whereas most companies in this space provide mere data, momentum, or benchmarking, we strive to provide true insight.
