A community of technologists, thinkers, and engineers. We solve problems based on our deep knowledge of the Datacenter and Cloud – through the cross section of business services, applications, servers, storage and the network infrastructure that connects it all.

Networking and IT Industry veterans founded FixStream Network, Inc. in May 2013 in order to build an in-depth data integration and analytics platform focused on Datacenter and Cloud management. Our technology transcends the entire domain to bring about the essential correlations that have never existed before – making silos redundant.
Datacenter and Cloud blind spots are Invasive, Pervasive and Expensive. Critical business services are increasingly architected as loosely coupled applications – running on physical and virtual infrastructure. Enterprises are moving their applications into virtualized and cloud environment very rapidly - and while that increases cost efficiency and business agility, it reduces visibility. Traps, obstacles, and lack of control are consistent – and it takes longer to isolate and identify any issues!

As the virtualization and Cloud trend will only grow from here, the challenge will get bigger and bigger. It will get increasingly difficult to monitor customer experience and mitigate business impacting performance problems. We need visibility from server, storage, network, flows, path and network services. Inability to see means inability to act!

Our Technology

What exactly do we Do?
We make sense out of massive amounts of disparate data – to solve complex Datacenter and Cloud problems using a fraction of time and resources. We enable simplistic visualization of these problems to create a application specific environment – putting the applications and business services at the center of it all! All this in real time!
How do we Do it?
We use deep-dive analytics in the Datacenter and Cloud environment. Keeping the business applications in the middle of the whole landscape - we integrate management data and operational logs to create complex, secure and collaborative analysis. This allows us to visualize business services, applications, servers, storage and networks, in all their complexity - in a single pane of glass!
How is this Made Possible?
With thousands of servers, services, applications, networking devices running together in a complex setting – each entity creates and stores management and operational logs. Our technology platform effectively manages this mountain of data – and correlate application instances, storage, flows, paths and network services.
