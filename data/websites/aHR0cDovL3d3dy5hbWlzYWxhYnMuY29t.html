Frequently Asked Questions
Q. How is AmisaDB different from other new search engine technologies? A. AmisaDB has been carefully architectured to unify the best of search engine, NoSQL and NewSQL database technologies to provide a comprehensive solution which can stand alone without having to integrate a database and search engine. We believe the best information retrieval is through massive parrallelism and in-memory indexes while the actual data is stored on flash or Disk. As such AmisaDB processes all SQL queries using a scalable in-memory inverted index without scanning any rows/documents to answer any query. If you seek the best performance for ad hoc queries or your application is under intensive writes, AmisaDB is the best tool for the job.
