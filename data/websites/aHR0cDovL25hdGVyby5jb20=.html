Finding answers in big data shouldn't require an advanced degree in computer science

Introducing a new way to analyze complex data

It's called Natero. A cloud-based platform that makes finding answers easier.

  • A cloud-based analytics solution as a service

    • Scale to billions of daily events seamlessly
    • Accessible from anywhere, shared by your team
    • Rapid deployment, get running in just a few days

  • A data platform for analysts, not just developers

    • Quickly add and track new metrics
    • Discover the events that lead to an action
    • Spend less time exploring and more time acting using advanced analytics

  • Data integration, analytics, and visualization together

    • Blend data from across all of your data sources
    • End-to-end automation at a button click
    • Connect with tools analysts know how to use

Analyzing your company’s data doesn’t have to be that hard.

We know data. And we know that much of the data a company generates (and absorbs) isn’t easily processed in existing systems. So it sits. And sits. Until it’s forgotten.

That’s where we come in. We help you organize the data you’ve been storing (and ignoring) in your corporate attic. We use the power of the cloud to help you collect, integrate and analyze your data, to liberate it, to turn it into knowledge and action.

With Natero, your product managers gain new insights about how your products are used, your analysts harness the value of your lost data, and your developers stay focused on what’s important to your business: building great products.
