What is a recommendation service?

Like "Customers Who Bought This Item Also Bought" or "What Other Items Do Customers Buy After Viewing This Item". recommendation can offer products that are highly likely to be of interest to the buyer by analyzing users' online activites.

Recommendation has become ubiquitous in the digital marketplace?

In Amazon, pioneer of eCommerce, 30% of its revenue generated come from on-site recommendations. Source: Building with big data

In Netflix, a leading online video streaming service provider, 60% of views result from recommendations. Source: Netflix Recommendations: Beyond the 5 stars (Part 1)

Easy to use?

Setting up a on-premise recommendation service is not easy. It takes big hardware, big data processing, advanced recommendation algorithm and so on.
If you are worried about the time and costs involved in developing on-premise recommendation service, RecoPick could be your answer!ㅁ

Just add a single code snippet and you're done. We do the rest.

Without additional works, simply insert a line of script to start data collection.
To apply the recommendation results to the site, all you have to do is add one javascript tag to the site pages.
RecoPick will handle the rest - data collection, processing algorithm application, and recommendation analysis(click rate, conversion rate).
