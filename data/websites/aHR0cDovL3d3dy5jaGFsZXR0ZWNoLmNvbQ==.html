Big Audit for Big Data.

 

Chalet Tech provides solutions to govern data transactions and improve business operations. The Big Audit platform makes it possible to account for, manage and deploy information just like any other vital business asset. A patented three-dimensional model automatically discovers, classifies and profiles data assets by time, space and state – creating a transparent audit trail for every end-to-end transaction to track data from conception to deletion. What is Big Data?

Big data is high volume, high velocity, and high variety information assets that demand cost-effective, innovative forms of information processing for enhanced insight and decision making.

Source: Gartner

What is Big Audit?

Big Audit is a software platform that customers use for the accounting, managing and deploying of information as they would any other physical business asset.

How do customers use Big Audit?

Users define policies within Big Audit to inspect every end-to-end data transaction to generate a complete audit trail of that data activity. Big Audit automatically discovers, classifies and profiles data assets. While doing so, Big Audit enforces policy and records details using all five elements of asset management - who, what, where, when, and how. 

So what?

There are many products available to analyze, report, and protect critical information. The problem is those products only give you single points of visibility and can take weeks to sift through piles of data to find exactly what is needed. They are limited by their physical & technological location in the enterprise and limited further by the silos of people who own them. Politics, budgets, and a large organization where people simply don't know each other are common hurdles preventing any chance of a total view of company data. Chalet Tech's patented technology provides that complete view and will neatly organize stored audit data for instant retrieval. Big Audit is the most robust data auditing solution available.
