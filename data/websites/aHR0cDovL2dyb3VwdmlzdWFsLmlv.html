About GroupVisual.io

GroupVisual.io is a user-interface and data visualization design consultancy. We believe that people should be empowered by data, to solve problems, make better decisions, and collaborate more effectively. We design user-interfaces and data experiences that help people gain actionable insights from their data. With more than 12 years of experience working with clients of every size, and across many industries, GroupVisual.io's multidisciplinary team brings cross-vertical domain and visualization expertise and fresh thinking to each client engagement.
