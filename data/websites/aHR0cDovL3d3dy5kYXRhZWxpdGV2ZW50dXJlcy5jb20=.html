About us

Up to now, the barrier of entry for the big data startups field was extraordinarily high. Founders with solid technology background struggled to transform their knowledge into useful product, and finding product-market fit in the Big Data space is challenging. Data-Elite is taking up the challenge by providing exceptional subject-matter mentorship from the best advisors in the field, superior funding from the Valley’s top investors, as well as office space to founders that have already proven their capacity to outperform conventional business challenges in the sector. Startups graduating from the program will be ready to disrupt and evolve an ever-evolving Big Data ecosystem.

Data Elite is an institution that provides world-class early stage funding, top tier counseling and a home to aspiring Big Data start-ups.
