Big Data for Retail

Big Data vs. Fraud
Wise Athena’s fraud detection service uses Big Data to provide companies with a powerful tool for identifying, fighting and significantly reducing fraud level. Our algorithms monitor business data 24/7, detecting anomalies accurately and stopping illegal transactions, while having minimal impact on lawful activities.

To keep the costs down, we offer on-demand delivery of fraud prediction and prevention service via the internet with a pay-as-you-go pricing option.

How We Do It

Remove uncertainty from decision making

Businesses generate large amounts of data through their day-to-day activities that is only valuable if transformed into actionable insights that are used to give a solid foundation to commercial decisions. Our Big Data cloud helps companies to remove intuition and uncertainty from key commercial areas by providing a sound base for crucial decision making.

No upfront investment and a quick set-up

Wise Athena applies economies of scale to Big Data services, keeping the costs low for customers, while offering a powerful platform capable of extracting all the value from business data.

Our quick set-up process means that your data will start working for you in a fraction of the time needed by traditional Big Data implementations.
Helping your business define
Big Data objectives
Breaking into new grounds brings new opportunities, but there are also risks, so it is crucial to ensure that the first steps are successful.

Big Data creates many new chances but you need to be aware of what you would like to achieve through advanced data analysis. Wise Athena will help you define goals and objectives first. This sets the success criteria and requirements for our business partnership. Wise Athena will open the way for you to new, exciting territories and provide both strategic and technical solutions.
A safe cloud for your data
The security challenges posed by collecting, storing and processing data are numerous and complex.  This is why we put security at the heart of our services, going well beyond the fundamentals required in a secure IT environment.

All our products are based on SAS 70 type II and US/EU certified services. In addition, we make sure that all of the data centres we work with are operating under the strictest control systems.
Wise tools for wiser analytics
Wise Athena follows the Software as a Service (SaaS) delivery model, in which software and data are centrally hosted in the cloud and are readily accessible from a web based interface. This approach allows our customers to focus on the insights drawn from the data while leaving hardware/software maintenance and support in our hands.
It’s all about results
Wise Athena offers more than actionable insights. We help to discover new, data-driven monetization opportunities that allow companies to leverage their existing customer base and raise their bottom line. Our proactive tools help to meet business challenges by translating data into effective commercial actions, such as highly targeted promotions and store location selection.
