Current Activities

We’re building some really cool technology here at Dragonfly…and we can’t wait until we can tell you all about it!

What we can tell you is that we are headquartered in Woburn Massachusetts, which is just north of Boston. We are proud to call the Boston area home as it is connected to many outstanding universities and talent.

Current Activities include establishing an architecture leadership center in the San Francisco Bay Area.  Stay tuned for future announcements.

Dragonfly Data Factory acquired facilities in Hyderabad and Visakhapatnam, India in October 2014. These facilities are now being designed and configured to host our software development center, data factory and business support services.
