Vision

Meet the Team

Lori Stewart

With more than 18 years of international technology and business experience, Lori is focused on helping entrepreneurs and development teams achieve their goals to create successful, world-changing software products.

Lori has held leadership and executive positions in strategy, product management and business development within software technology companies in Canada and the US (eBay, EZ-Robot, and Zazz) and is an advisor to many young startups.

Lori led the International Product team at eBay which delivered the eBay platform and online payment solutions to 22 countries in 2 years and she was an advisor to the executive team on international strategy.
Additionally, she was a key member of the half.com and Paypal acquisition teams, as well as delivering eBay Stores, Buy it Now and other products to the eBay global platform and holds patents that deliver search functionality and predictive buying patterns for eBay customers around the world.
In addition, Lori actively helps nurture and mentor the next generation of entrepreneurs in many ways – She is the founder of the Awesome Foundation, Calgary chapter, a worldwide network of people devoted to micro-philanthropy and she is a frequent judge, mentor and speaker at startup and tech events in Canada and the US.
Lori holds a degree in Economics from Carleton University in Ottawa and is certified in Product Management and Project Management. She is a member of the A100 (Alberta’s Tech Entrepreneurs), ADG (Alberta Deal Generator) and Energy Futures Network and sits on the editorial board of TheOGM, (Oil and Gas Magazine) as well as, the Deputy Premier’s expert panel on the future of Innovation in Alberta.

 

Jagadish Channagiri,
CEO & Founder

Jagadish has 20+ years’ experience as a Programmer, Executive, and Entrepreneur. Prior to Moogilu, he was the VP Engineering/Operations at Bittorrent. He led the team that developed the Bittorent Clients and

Infrastructure. He pioneered local search at GeoTouch where he was the CTO and VP Engineering. He has worked as a senior executive and engineer at Intel, AMD, Oracle, and Mentor Graphics.

As an entrepreneur he founded Powdeal. At Powdeal he built an Integrated Retail Marketing Platform on Web, Social media, and Mobile.

 

Riad Hartani,
Advisor

Dr Hartani has over 20 years ICT experience including executive positions at leading Silicon Valley Startups (Wichorus, Anagran, Caspian); Lead positions in various multinationals (Nortel Canada, Hitachi

Japan, GE France); and Lead Researcher in prestigious research organizations (CNRS France, NRC Canada, CRL Japan).

He is also a founding member of technology startups in Silicon Valley and a technical advisor /consultant to several internet and media ventures in the US, Far East and the Middle East.

His major interests include cloud architectures, cloud assessment, LTE/4G strategies, networking systems design, networking architectures, internet protocols development and artificial intelligence. Dr Hartani is also an active contributor to the IETF, ATMF, ITU-T, OIF, IEEE and the IPv6 Forum and a frequent invited speaker and panelist at various international industry events.

Dr Hartani has been lead design of NGN projects for several large operators such as NTT and KT; and IT architecture for large enterprises such as Aramco and HKEx.

Riad has obtained Doctoral and Post Doctoral degrees at University of Paris (Computer Science with highest honors) and University of California Berkeley, and Executive Business Education at Stanford University.
