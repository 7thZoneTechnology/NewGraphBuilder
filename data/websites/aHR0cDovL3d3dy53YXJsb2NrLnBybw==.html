What is creative DNA?

  • Every ad is built from DNA parts: words, sentences and visuals that together create promises and insights. Sometimes one word or even a face expression in a picture can create a dramatic change in the impact an ad has on the consumer, therefore on results. We call those DNA parts “Impact Factors”.
    Turning art into science, Warlock is a first of its kind creative analytics and optimization platform that developed an extensive genome structure, in order to help advertisers get smarter with creative. Using the Warlock platform will help you get answers to some of the questions every brand faces:

    • Why makes a customer click on your ad and convert?
    • How can you attract the right customer to respond?
    • How can you use creative tactics and techniques to improve your business KPIs?
    • How can you keep your campaign performance level when ads performance erodes?

Creative analytics

  • Through the lifespan of a brand, hundreds of ads are created, some of them more successful, some less. The problem is that no one can really tell what made a specific ad work better than the other; no one has found a way to systematically harness the influence of the creative element in large scale campaigns.

    Warlock rises to the challenge, combining wide-ranging advertising expertise, math and big data in order to crack the “creative code” for generating engagement. Through big data analytics and pattern recognition we realize the key impact factors (conceptual, grammatical and visual) that influence audiences, and map them in order to achieve better performance, in any digital media format, time after time.

The Warlock Solutions

  • Creative analytics

    The Warlock platform will analyze all of your brand’s creative work and campaign data, ‘extract the most important creative impact factors that influence success (conceptual, grammatical and visual) and give you extensive reports that will guide you to improve campaign performance and the effectiveness of your ads. By using the Warlock dashboard, you’ll get active tracking of every creative impact factor that influences your campaign, including any new ad that will be aired. Moreover you’ll be able to juxtapose between any impact factor and the ads’ targeting, so you know what message works best for which target audience in any territory and act accordingly for better results.

    Ad Creation

    Warlock can also provide you with new “coded & trigger oriented” ads built with the knowledge that the system acquired during the analytics stage. Those ads will be specifically created, not just to gain more success, but also to discover more ‘key motivators’ that will make your customers react.
