  • Contactive Lands on Amazon’s Fire Phone



    We’re excited to announce that you can now download Contactive for the Amazon Fire Phone! All of the benefits of Contactive’s universal caller ID are now available to Amazon Fire Phone users, giving them full profiles of whoever is calling, even if the number is not in their address book. In addition to the Fire Phone’s intuitive Firefly capabilities that allow users to identify thousands of movies, urls, household products, and more, Contactive ensures you’ll never be without the information you need in the moments that matter most.

    With Contactive for Amazon Fire you’ll see a brand new user interface specifically designed to integrate with your home screen. You’ll be able to scroll through widgets that provide you with important Contactive updates without ever needing to navigate into the app. Some of the info you’ll see with this feature includes real-time social media updates, recent call history, birthday notifications, and blocked spammers. You’ll also be able to see Yelp information for any incoming call from a Yelp business.
