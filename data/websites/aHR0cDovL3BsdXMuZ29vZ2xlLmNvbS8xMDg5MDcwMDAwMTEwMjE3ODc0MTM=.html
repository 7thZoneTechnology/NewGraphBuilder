Über mich
SalesPredict helps B2B companies increase revenues by identifying who their real potential buyers are and providing information and insights that accelerate sales cycles and improve conversion rates. How? Predictive analytics + your CRM and marketing automation system data + signals from the web and hundreds of outside data sources.

The insights that SalesPredict delivers can help your team have more impactful interactions with prospects throughout the customer lifecycle so you maximize revenue at every phase.

With SalesPredict you can:
  • Increase your qualified lead pipeline 2x
  • Double your conversion rates
  • Accelerate your sales cycle by 30% or more
  • Reduce customer churn by up to 25%
Learn more at www.salespredict.com.
