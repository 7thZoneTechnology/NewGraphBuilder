360° analytical views

Today the most successful companies use internal and external data effectively and can drive competitive advantage by converting data into insight. Our modular services platform enable agile approach whether you are in a quest for improving your customer or operations analytics. We support you in combining traditional data sources such ERP and CRM with other data that is available now - in the era of big data.

Lets together make sure that you manage and consume the data in a manner which enables you to get the most value out of it!

Customer Analytics

Do you want to increase your share of wallet or a customer lifetime value within an existing customer base and find new prospects. With our service platform designed for customer analytics, you will get a better visibility internally and externally while increasing your understanding about your customers’ needs. We do this by combining marketing automation information with website visitor, customer and financials data. Ad-hoc analytics and self-service Business Intelligence capabilities empower you and your colleagues to do better decisions based on real insight.

What We Do

Our customers are companies that possess or could possess huge amount of machine data, have special requirements regards to real-time data management or would like to increase their maturity in information driven decision making. We support our customers in capitalising the data and combine it with other information in order to produce improved business insight.

Therefore our customers need a partner who can deliver them modular services and expertise that support them in their quest towards holistic 360° views.

Data Management

Our cloud based data management service platform is designed for real-time data management. The service supports time series data up to microsecond time points and can consume efficiently also unstructured data. Our service platform is flexible and scales according to your needs and requirements. You have always an explicit SLA behind the service delivery.

On-premise hardware and configurations become obsolete. No need for new investments. The service is easy to budget and you get regular reports with your monthly service bill.

Condition Monitoring

Want to make sure that a production line or a process is running as planned. Our condition monitoring services are designed for to detect anomalous behaviour and will trigger an alarm or a notification if a such condition takes place. In addition to providing condition monitoring services for the data that resides in our data management platform we can also extend the condition monitoring service to cover processes that reside on your premises.

Diagnostics and Analytics

The real business value is produced via personalised and dynamic analytical views and reports. This requires that the data is dimensionally modelled and the source system integrations are designed to support the needed reporting and analytic capabilities. As no business is static and the business climate is in continuous transformation, the service framework needs to be flexible as well as scalable. This is achieved trough cloud driven platform.
