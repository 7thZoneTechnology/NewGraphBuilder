FlyData Inc. is a cloud-based data integration company based in Mountain View, CA. Our mission is to provide “a data pump to the cloud". We are currently focusing on providing services that sync with Amazon Redshift, a data warehousing service optimized for storing and querying big data.

Redshift is a revolutionary product that will change how big data analytics will work for many years to come, but there are many difficulties in terms of data extraction, transformation, and loading (ETL). To mitigate these issues, FlyData offers services that will ease the migration of your data onto Redshift. Our solutions include automatic and rapid transfer of log data onto Redshift (FlyData Autoload), as well as an add-on to help keep track of Heroku App Logs (FlyData for Heroku). If you collect big data in MySQL or MariaDB, FlyData Sync can replicate this data onto Redshift for easy analysis.

Formerly known as Hapyrus in Japan, FlyData Inc. was founded in 2011 after being funded by prominent investors, notably 500 Startups and CyberAgent Ventures. For more information, check out our website at https://www.flydata.com/. Check out our blog at http://www.flydata.com/blog and follow @FlyDataInc on Twitter! We are an official partner of Amazon Redshift.

Specialties

Enterprise Data Integration, Amazon Redshift, Data Integration, Big Data, Integration Platform as a Service (iPaaS), B2B, Amazon Web Services, Cloud, Amazon S3, MySQL, Heroku, Databases
