Connecting Ideas, Data and Knowledge

We build tools to make "Big Data" in the life sciences work for you. For making business critical decisions, its not only about how deep you go into a dataset for insights, it's about how quickly you connect the dots across very diverse data sources. These connections allow you to identify cause-and-effect relationships that influence your bottom-line - in business or scientific research.

At Metaome, we understand this. We believe that all the data in the world should be connected; and all the connections must be easily navigable by you and me. We make this possible through our proprietary platform, DistilBio that seamlessly combines an intuitive interface with powerful graph based search and data integration. Gain actionable insights from data that you have always had by installing DistilBio on a secure private cloud within your organization. Alternatively, explore our online version that connects across biological databases in the public domain.
