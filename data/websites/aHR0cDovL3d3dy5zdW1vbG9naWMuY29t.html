What is Cloud Log Management?

A cloud-based log management service eliminates the operational overhead and complexity of today's on-premise log management solutions. On-premise solutions quickly run into scalability issues with increasing log volumes, and logs are voluminous. A cloud native solution with its elastic scalability enables you to process ALL your log data with zero extra overhead. This scalability also powers interactive analytics that will deliver answers from your logs in seconds.
