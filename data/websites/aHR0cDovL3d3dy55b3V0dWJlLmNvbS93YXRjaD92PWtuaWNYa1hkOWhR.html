Hochgeladen am 20.10.2010

The Second Annual GPU Technology Conference, held from Sept. 20-23, brought together participants from 50 countries to hear more than 270 hours of technical sessions on a wide range of high-performance computing and scientific research subjects.

ParStream, our GPU-based parallel database, convinced the jury and allowed us to become the first non-US company to receive this award in recent years. For us this is a very profound acknowledgment of our research and development work. It motivates us to continue on our path to:
Solve the problem of analyzing mass data in real-time for all industries.

Proceeding from the GPU Technology Conference 2010 (www.nvidia.com/gtc). Displayed with permission from NVIDIA.
