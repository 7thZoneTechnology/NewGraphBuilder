Über mich
Hi-Tech provides cost effective and high quality, state-of-the-art Computer Aided Designing and Drafting (CADD) services through its offshore facilities in India.

We are your one-stop solution for specialized services in the domain of Architectural, Structural, Electrical, Mechanical and Electronics engineering. Our cutting edge technological expertise can meet your requirements ranging from 2D Drafting, 3D Modeling, Designing to Animation and Walkthrough.

List of Services:

- Architectural Design Services
- Structural Design Services
- Mechanical Design Services
- MEP Design Services
- BIM Services
- Energy Modeling Services – Building Energy Analysis

Hi-Tech is staffed by highly skilled, computer savvy and English speaking professionals. Our work culture is based on a positive attitude and strong values, which contribute to our strong performance, productivity and retention.

Specialties: CAD Design & Drafting, 3D CAD Modeling & Rendering, FEA/CFD/CAE Analysis & Simulation, BIM Design, Drafting & Modeling, Energy Modeling (LEED) & Simulation, MEP Design, Drafting & Modeling, 3D Product Design & Modeling, Reverse Engineering & 3D Laser Scanning
