The Big Data revolution has created a diverse set of companies focused on solving complex Big Data problems. Antuit was set up with a somewhat different purpose – help enterprises use data to solve business problems and refine intuitive business decisions through a deliberate methodology that harnesses both left and right brain thinking. We start with our clients’ intuition and points of view on their core business challenges. From there, we leverage our unique combination of domain expertise, data science skills and execution experience to create relevant, actionable answers. We then work closely with our clients to help drive implementation and realize value.
But we don’t stop at one business problem. Over time, our model is designed to:

  • Migrate clients to world-class standards of data usage in decision making today.
  • Build capabilities for a future where insights from earned data through social media and digital interaction will effectively drive prescriptive decision making.
