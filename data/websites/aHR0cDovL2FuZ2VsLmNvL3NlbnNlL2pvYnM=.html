Product

We’re building the right way for teams and enterprise to do data science — a radically better data science platform that supports the most powerful tools available and is delivered at a fraction of the cost of traditional solutions. Both data scientists and CTOs love us. We're just getting started.

Sense was founded by two PhDs with proven experience building sophisticated data science software. The company is advised by Microsoft's former Chief Economist and leading academics at Stanford.

Why Us?

We're building the best platform for data science to tackle the toughest problems in science and business. If you like working on hard problems with smart, creative, and dedicated people, Sense is for you.
