HitFox Group is a Platform for Entrepreneurship focused on AdTech, FinTech & Big Data industries. We are committed to agile working processes and pragmatism. Our businesses are built with cloud services and modern technologies, e.g. Ruby on Rails. We are 300 professionals of 40 nationalities headquartered in Berlin, with additional offices in San Francisco and Seoul. HitFox is founding 4-6 new companies every year and we are continuously looking for talented people who want to join our Fox team and build great companies together.

For more insight on career opportunities and life at HitFox, check out: http://vimeo.com/99734712 and http://www.hitfoxgroup.com/jobs.html

Our portfolio companies are: ad2games, appiris, AppLift, datamonk, PubNative, plus five more companies currently in stealth mode. HitFox was founded in 2011 by serial entrepreneurs Jan Beckers, Dr. Hanno Fichtner, Tim Koschella and Ruben Haas.
