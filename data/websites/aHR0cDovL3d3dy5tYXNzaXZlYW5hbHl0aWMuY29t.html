We are an innovation company developing Oscar Bao, globally scalable business analytic optimization platform (BAO) for the big data era. We were awarded a SMART innovation grant by the UK Technology Strategy Board. Four patents have been filed to date to protect our unique intellectual property.

Oscar is next generation Business Analytics Optimization. It’s really intuitive and easy to use and you don’t have to be a business intelligence specialist, analyst or statistician to understand how it works. But if you are that’s ok too, because we have ‘Super User’ and ‘Analyst’ editions of Oscar Bao designed especially for you launching next year, so you won’t have long to wait. If you’re a business user, with just a little training you’ll soon be joining and turning structured and unstructured data into actionable insights, discovering them in massive datasets, predicting, forecasting and gauging your customers’ sentiments, all through elegant engaging infographic storydashboards, all without ever needing IT support. And you’ll be doing it much, much faster than you ever thought possible. And that’s our promise.

Oscar is the only SaaS, fully featured BAO suite for big data and is available as ‘Business User’ Edition Beta trial.

We are also developing hyperfast data movement and access technology that uses ground-breaking information density holography to make your big data portable so you can move really big data and even stream video really fast.

Together Oscar and IDH lets you compress, move, and access, analyse and store data better than any other suite of products. They are easy to install and do not require extensive technology integration, change and program management.
