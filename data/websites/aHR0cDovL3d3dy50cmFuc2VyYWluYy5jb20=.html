About Transera

Transera is leading entry into a new era for contact centers, where “Big Data” technologies delivered via the cloud are leveraged to connect the huge volumes of structured and unstructured data contact centers collect through the course of their daily operations and then mine it to improve business performance. Transera offers the first cloud-based software for organizations to understand the true context of every interaction and then take the most effective course of action to achieve better business outcomes throughout the customer lifetime journey.
