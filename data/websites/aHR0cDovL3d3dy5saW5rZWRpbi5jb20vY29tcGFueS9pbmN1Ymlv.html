Incubio is a startup business incubator that specializes in the creation of projects that offer business processes as a service, with the use of Big Data technologies. Founded in 2011 in Silicon Valley, it now operates from Barcelona.

Our methods have a very practical academic format and are limited to projects that stand out - thanks to the use of Big Data technologies. We support entrepreneurs from the concept development phase to the private investment phase, in addition to the search for public financing and all procedures that are considered to be of minimal business value but indispensible for the success of the project; such as the makeup of society or the physical infrastructure of the office, so that the entrepreneur can make the best use of their time and maximize their talents.

We watch over our little ecosystem of incubating companies with great care so that we can help them to grow by taking on all of their costs, and even finding financing, be it public or private, and by using our experience to guide them on their path. This makes Incubio an invaluable tool for entrepreneurs looking to start a company of their own.
