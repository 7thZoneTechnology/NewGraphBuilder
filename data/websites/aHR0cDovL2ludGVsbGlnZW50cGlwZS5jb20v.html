Mission

Intelligentpipe is a Big Data SW Company for serving Telecommunications Industry and developing products for Mobile Operators to improve their business and operational processes.


About the company

Intelligentpipe was founded in 2012. The company has tens of years experience in Mobile Telecommunications Industry. Offices are located in Tampere, FINLAND and in SINGAPORE.
