What is Food Genius?

Insights for Foodservice

Food Genius collects and synthesizes massive amounts of restaurant industry data - including menu descriptions and item prices - and creates actionable insights. Insights are delivered to clients through a variety of platforms complete with identifiable market opportunities, emerging restaurant menu trend statistics, implementable strategies rooted in nuanced market-level data, and detailed, data-enhanced sales and marketing collateral.


Equipping clients for success in a competitive market.
