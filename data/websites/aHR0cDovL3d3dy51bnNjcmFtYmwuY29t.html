Evolving Insights

Company

Uncover insights from your Big Data, even when you’re not looking for them.

As enterprises tread down the Big Data highway, they are keenly interested in uncovering actionable as well as surprising insights from it. The traditional way of accessing Big Data is by querying it or visualizing it in different forms. This is limited to the degree that you have to figure out what you are looking for before you find the answer.

At Unscrambl, we take the next leap. We are building a platform that will automatically analyze your Big Data to serendipitously discover insights. We take some basic hints from you on the kinds of data at your disposal and your general topics of interest and then we get to work. We’ll answer questions you never knew you had.

Product

The core premise of Big Data is simple : Data meets Analytics to create Insights. The main question is: what kinds of Data and what kinds of Analytics will give you what kinds of Insights? The key innovation of Unscrambl is a platform that can automatically apply relevant analytics to the right data to create insights. Depending on the quality and usefulness of the insights, we can either further dig deeper and refine these insights or explore new data and analytic combinations.

The kinds of data and analytics available in many industries is mind-boggling. However, while that is seen as a challenge by some, we look at it as an opportunity. We love the combinatorial explosion when we combine n types of data with m types of analytics and keep applying this recursively. Hidden in this explosion is the needle that we seek to uncover with minimal human guidance.

InfoSphere Streams

IBM® InfoSphere® Streams is an advanced analytic platform that allows user-developed applications to quickly ingest, analyze and correlate information as it arrives from thousands of real-time sources. Unscrambl team members have unparalleled experience in building various kinds of real-time analytic applications on Streams and optimizing them to handle very high data throughput rates, while preserving extensibility, fault-tolerance, high availability and security.

InfoSphere BigInsights

IBM® BigInsights® makes it simpler for people to use Hadoop and build Big Data applications. It enhances the Hadoop open source technology to withstand the demands of your enterprise, adding administrative, discovery, development, provisioning and security features, along with best-in-class analytical capabilities from IBM Research. We have extensive experience in building solutions using various components of BigInsights, including Hadoop, HBase, Hive, BigSQL, BigSheets and R.

Apache Hadoop

The Apache Hadoop is an open source software framework that allows for the distributed processing of large data sets across clusters of computers using a simple programming model. Unscrambl team members have published extensively on topics related to the Hadoop programming model and task scheduling in Hadoop. Clients will benefit from our team’s intimate knowledge of Hadoop and the accompanying hands-on experience.

Apache Storm

Apache Storm is a an open source distributed real-time computation system. Storm makes it easy to reliably process unbounded streams of data, doing for real-time processing what Hadoop did for batch processing. Storm is fairly simple to program and can be used with any programming language. Recently, Storm has been been accepted to Apache’s incubator program; an indicator of the support that Storm has garnered in the computing community.
