Tons of Features

Velos provides a complete enterprise workflow management system for Predictive Analytics, enabling your data analysts and engineers to quickly deploy business-impacting Predictive Analytics.

Integrate Your Data Sources

Whether you are using local files, SQL databases, or HDFS, Velos lets you quickly integrate your all data sources.

Model Selection & Evaluation

With Velos, selecting and evaluating models has never been easier or faster. Velos lets you very quickly choose the optimal model for your use case.

Transform Your Data

Velos provides automated ETL tools, to help you quickly transform, join, merge, and prepare your data for predictive modeling.

Team Collaboration

Velos provides built-in functionality to allow your data analyst and science teams to communicate and track activity within and across projects.

Automated Feature Engineering

Velos provides easy-to-use, automated feature selection and creation tools, allowing you to quickly find the optimal features for your model.

Deployment & Operations

Velos lets you go from model selection to a live, fully-functional model in just minutes. You can also set operational schedules for re-training and adapting your production models.
