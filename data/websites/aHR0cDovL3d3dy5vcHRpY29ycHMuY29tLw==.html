The increasing demand for big data combined with the rapid adoption of mobile devices and the associated service offerings such as streaming video, TV-on-Demand, gaming, social media, and other high data capacity offerings has led to an explosion in internet traffic and demand for data.  Fiber Optics is the only technology available to meet the high demand requirements of big data, devices and services.

To meet this need, Opticorps has positioned itself to deliver exceptional, quality based Last Mile fiber optic network splicing and connection services.  We provide customer facing, soft-skills based, quality oriented services for FTTH, MDUâs, FTB, FTTP and FTTx.  Our services adhere to all ANSI, IEC, TIA, NEC, NESC and Telcordia standards.

Opticorps talented, experienced professional staff are dedicated to superior quality, integrity and reliability for fiber optic network splicing and connection services.  With our state of the art splicing and test equipment, Opticorps is well-positioned to service our customers. Based on customer requirements, we mobilize our technical teams anywhere across the United States. Whether servicing Government Agencies, Communications Carriers, Broadband Service Providers, Wireless Service Providers, Internet Service Providers, Cable TV Companies, Utility Companies for Gas, Hydro, Rail or Transit, or Industrial, Commercial, Medical and Educational Enterprises, Opticorps is dedicated to servicing our customers. 

Opticorps' name was derived by combining the words Optical and Corps, which is representative of the Marine Corps ability to deploy and mobilize.  At Opticorps we deploy and mobilize anywhere across the United States.
