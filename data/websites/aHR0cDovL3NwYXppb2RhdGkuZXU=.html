We open source & open data

Open refine

We added dataTXT to the Open Refine Named-Entity Recognition extension developed by the awesome Free Your Metadata team.
Enrich, improve, analyze, contextualize your CSV and Excel data with semantic entities from DBpedia directly within Open Refine!

DBpedia

DBpedia is an international, open, collaborative effort to extract data from Wikipedia and make it available as Linked Data.
We developed and maintain the Italian DBpedia in collaboration with the Web of Data Unit of the Fondazione Bruno Kessler and several other contributors.

Wikisearch

We developed Wikisearch: a semantic search API that helps you find the specific Wikipedia page you’re looking for.
If you don’t remember its exact title, or have only a vague remembrance that it relates to some specific topic, this is the API you are looking for!
