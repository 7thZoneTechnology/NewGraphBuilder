Document Management in Melbourne

Cost effective and secure document management solutions

Micro Image provides document management solutions across Melbourne. Imagine instant access to your invoices from wherever you are, all your medical records stored securely online, or even an automated accounts payable process. It’s all possible with Micro Image’s document management solutions.

We provide secure document management services in Melbourne for clients big and small. We offer document scanning solutions, data capture services and electronic document storage solutions that will organise, streamline and secure your information and reduce the need to retain your paper documents. We also offer large format scanning for converting documents like maps and blueprints into an easy to manage digital form.

Micro Image has proven document management systems for:

  • Managing invoices and accounts, claims processing
  • Fast and easy compliance for legal, tax and financial records
  • Archiving client and medical records
  • Processing inbound mail and electronic documents
  • Storing archived documents and images
  • Creating efficient digital document processes

With over 30 years’ experience covering many industries, business sizes and project specifications, we are able to create an affordable solution to suit your exact needs.

Contact us today to discuss how we can optimise your organisation’s document management systems.

Micro Image can help you to:

  • Find and manage documents quickly and easily

  • Reduce storage space and costs

  • Implement a solution specific to your document workflows

  • Less paper and less time managing paper
