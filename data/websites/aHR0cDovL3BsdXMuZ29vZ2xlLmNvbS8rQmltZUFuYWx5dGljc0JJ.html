Über mich
Bime is a pioneering  cloud BI solution, born in and built for the cloud. Bime delivers simple-to-use yet powerful BI, analytics, data visualization and dashboarding in a software-as-a-service application, making data analysis fast, easy and low-cost. 

We Are Cloud, the company behind Bime, was founded in 2009 in Montpellier, an academic R&D hotbed in the South of France . We Are Cloud has been recognized as a trailblazer in the cloud BI field, most recently as “Best European Start Up of the Year” at ICT Spring 2011 in Luxembourg and “Best Cloud Application” by the Cloud Computing World Series Awards in London. 

In September we launched our latest innovation, Bime V3.0 with QueryBlender, at the DEMO Fall 2011 event in California.
