Make smarter decisions and win more customers with SAP InfiniteInsight for predictive analytics

With traditional predictive analytics, you can expect to spend a great deal of time on activities that are manual, repetitive, and prone to human error. SAP InfiniteInsight (formerly KXEN) has changed all that – automating most of the effort so that users can gain unprecedented customer insight and make forward-looking decisions with ease.

  • Make predictive analytics accessible to a broad spectrum of users in real operational environments
  • Automate data prep, predictive modeling, and deployment tasks
  • Build sophisticated predictive models in minutes or hours, not weeks or months
  • Scale for petabytes of Big Data and gain rapid insight from thousands of variables
  • Embed predictive analytics into your business apps, processes, information
  • Seamlessly access SAP InfiniteInsight through SAP Customer Engagement Intelligence to build better real-time customer segmentations

What's Included

Collapse All

  • Predictive Modeling’s First-Ever Semantic Layer

    Define a broad selection of reusable business components that can be applied over and over again – so you can automatically create analytical data sets for predictive modeling. This innovative approach is dramatically faster than traditional, handcrafted techniques.

    • Build reusable analytical records to create unlimited analytical data sets
    • Describe thousands of attributes comprising your interest domains
    • Define aggregates and attributes with time dependencies – only once
    • Optimize scoring equations for major databases such as Teradata, Oracle, SQL Server, IBM DB2, and Netezza
    Less

  • Build Accurate Models in Hours

    As a business analyst, you don’t want to have to worry about complicated math or which algorithm to use. Designed for accuracy and optimal results, SAP InfiniteInsight automates the process of building sophisticated predictive models for every major data mining function.

    • Benefit from an easy-to-use, automated approach to modeling
    • Supports classification, regression, segmentation, forecasting, and association rule functions
    • Get fast, best-in-class modeling results with built-in robustness and accuracy
    • Obtain summary reports to communicate model characteristics and findings
    • Scales to thousands of variables and millions of transactions
    Less

  • Put Scores into Action

    Building a great predictive model is only the first step. To get business results, you still need to deploy it into production. With clicks, not code, SAP InfiniteInsight allows you to deploy optimized scoring equations right into your database.

    • Automatically generate bulletproof scoring code
    • Integrate scoring in a variety of formats, such as SAS, Java, C, and PMML
    • Optimized code for maximum performance – including structure and sequence of calculations
    • Optimize scoring equations for all major databases such as Teradata, Oracle, Sybase, and Netezza
    Less

  • Predictions at Peak Performance

    Traditional predictive methods make it impossible to optimize a lot of campaigns or to refresh models as needed. It simply takes too long. SAP InfiniteInsight gives you a faster way to build, deploy, and improve any number of predictive models.

    • Benefit from a browser-based, authenticated environment
    • Leverage a user-friendly scheduling interface designed for business analysts
    • Receive alerts to allow for “management by exception” of data and model deviations
    • Obtain reports adapted to different user profiles – including analyst, supervisor, and administrator
    • Maintain an unlimited number of predictive models

    Less

  • Gain Insight into Your Social Networks

    It’s no secret that people socialize in groups. By using SAP InfiniteInsight Social you can analyze business data to learn about the links between your customers and which customers have a strong social influenceSocial network analysis can provide predictive insight to create a competitive advantage across your business including viral marketing campaigns.

    • Gain end-to-end social network analysis capabilities
    • Use powerful visualization capabilities and graph exploration
    • Natively integrate social attributes into predictive models
    • Identify “influencers” and node segmentation for specific domains
    • Detect hidden links in your data and connect individuals using multiple identities
    Less

  • Recommendations Powered by Predictive Analytics

    Provide personalized recommendations to each unique website visitor with SAP InfiniteInsight Recommendation. Many sites lack recommendation capabilities relying on “bestseller” rules and simplistic approaches to personalize content and product recommendations.

    • Analyze and weight any combination of transactional data sources
    • Build for Big Data, such as clickstreams, with virtually unlimited rows and columns
    • Visualize and explore recommendation rule-sets
    • Easily calculate real-time recommendations
    • Deploy recommendations in your production apps (website, mobile, etc.)
    Less
