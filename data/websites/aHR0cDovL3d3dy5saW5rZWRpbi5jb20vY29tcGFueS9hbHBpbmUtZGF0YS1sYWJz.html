Alpine Data Labs offers the world’s first script-less and web-based advanced analytics software for Big Data and Hadoop.

With Alpine, Data Scientists and Business Analysts can work with large data sets, develop, refine models and collaborate at scale without having to use code or download software. Leaders in all industries, from Financial Services to Healthcare, use Alpine to outsmart their competition.

Maybe you should too: find out more at www.alpinedatalabs.com.
