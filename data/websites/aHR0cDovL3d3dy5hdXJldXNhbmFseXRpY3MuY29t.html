Powering Big Data Analytics at the Point of Decision

Aureus Statistical and Analytical Platform (ASAP) is an integrated Big Data Analytics platform which eliminates the need to have multiple tools for understanding your Customers, Risks to Business and Operational efficiencies.

ASAP provides your enterprise a single window entry into the wide and complex world of Big Data Analytics.It is designed to be an integrated analytics platform that can work across various types of data stores and structures - structured and unstructured; internal and external; and big and small. With its supervised and unsupervised learning skills, the analytics platform makes it easier for business users (CEOs, CMOs, etc...) and analysts to work with the data, rather than on the data.

ASAP is a unique Big Data Analytics platform that combines industry specific best practices with relevant Data, Statistical & Analytical Models thus enabling organizations to rapidly deploy Big Data Analytics solutions across the organization in extremely compressed timelines.

Business Specific products for smarter point of decision Analytics

Each of Aureus' products have been developed to solve unique problems for specific industries, for example Insurance Analytics.

These products are based on complex analytical and statistical models that take into account challenges faced by Customer, Operations and Risk Management teams in Insurance, Banking and Healthcare sectors.
