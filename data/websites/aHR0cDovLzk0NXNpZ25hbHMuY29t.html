MyGovIn

A Vertical search engine for Indian Government sites/Services. When a user is looking for a particular government service, it turns out that it is not particularly easy to find. Many of the government web sites simply enumerate the available services in a list organized by categories. Public’s interaction with the MyGovin allows people quickly directing visitors to the services or information they are seeking. MyGovin is a platform that provides entry point of both central and state government services. Providing easy to use, location independent, 24/7 access to Government Services.

The various Government Departments such as Central, State and Local collects, use and manage large volumes of information/services and to ensure effective access to government policymaking and operations as well as to ensure government accountability, the public needs consistent, predictable access to government information. Public’s interaction with the MyGovin allows people quickly directing visitors to the services or information they are seeking.
