Qumram - The Big Data platform
for compliant cross-channel recording, e-discovery and user experience intelligence to empower your digital business.

Qumram seamlessly records and plays back all online information and communication in all relevant channels – Web, Social and Mobile. Furthermore Qumram replays real user interactions and transactions at any given point in time.

This enables you to create a single source of truth for all your online channels. Build a trusted environment for governance and risk management. Create a foundation for true end-to-end online processes and predictive big data analytics.

Our superior technology empowers efficient and accountable digital business all around the globe.
