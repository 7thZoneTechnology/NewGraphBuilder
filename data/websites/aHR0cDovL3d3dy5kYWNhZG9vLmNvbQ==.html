Health Score Explained

Track and benchmark your health and well-being in an easy and fun way with the dacadoo Health Score. It is a number from 1 (low) to 1,000 (high) and based on who you are, how you feel and how you live your life. When tracked over time, it offers a good directional indicator of how your health and well-being is evolving.
