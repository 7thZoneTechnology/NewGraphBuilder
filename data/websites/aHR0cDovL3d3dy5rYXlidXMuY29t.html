Use Cases for Knowledge Automation – Optimizing the Front Office

Knowledge is a powerful strategic asset harnessed appropriately. Our customers are seeing huge business benefits by dramatically shortening the time it takes to get everyone on the same page.

One manufacturer reduced the time to launch new products by 75% leveraging Kaybus to get the word out. They are now able to move fast by measuring who is ready with the new product details and who isn’t isolating challenges and taking real time action.

One Service center customer reduced call hold time by 40%. They attributed most of this impact to reducing the time it takes to ensure everyone knows how to handle a new problem when one is discovered by quickly deploying new solutions via Kaybus.
