What is CareConnectors

CareConnectors is a business-to-business healthcare company that is enabling the
PatientGraph ™. Our technology enables patients, healthcare providers and healthcare enterprises to exchange traditional and non-traditional healthcare data in a secure, HIPAA compliant ecosystem, including support for the workflow used every day by providers. Our healthcare provider and enterprise customers use PatientGraph Connect ™ and our related PatientGraph API ™ to deliver personalized, affordable health outcomes based on a comprehensive view of PatientGraph Life ™ that extends beyond traditional medical data to include behavior, social and learning data sets.

How the PatientGraph Platform Works

At CareConnectors, we are solving the big problem of two-way healthcare data exchange by making it possible for developers, enterprises and providers to share and exchange healthcare data without having to understand HL7 interfaces or the complex inner-workings of nearly 1,000 different medical record systems deployed in varying configurations by different health systems in the United States. The PatientGraph Platform consists of 3 components:

1
Bi-directional PatientGraph Connectors - We enable secure communication between medical record keeping systems and third-party applications, services and devices. This is our "secret sauce" or magic and is covered, in part, by various patents. We make it easy for trusted parties, including patients, to share healthcare data with authorized providers, devices and services.

2
Public PatientGraph APIs - We provide APIs for developers, enterprises and providers to insure that healthcare data exchange requirements can be adapted to each PatientGraph customer without requiring any customization to the target medical record keeping system as configured by each healthcare provider or system. The APIs also insure that the provider level workflow remains unchanged - the people who practice medicine every day do not have to learn how to use new functionality or a new interface.

3.
PatientGraph SDKs - We provide OS and service specific SDKs for developers, enterprises and providers to rapidly prototype, test and trial their healthcare devices, applications and services.
