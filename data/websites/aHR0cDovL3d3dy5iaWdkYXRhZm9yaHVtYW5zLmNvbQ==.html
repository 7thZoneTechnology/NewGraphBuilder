Introducing The Customer Graph

Having helped many of the world’s leading online brands implement customer centric marketing strategies, we often find it surprisingly difficult to answer the questions of who the brand's customers are; what do they want and how much they are worth. Where answers exist they lack the clarity, specificity and timeliness to support effective digital marketing plans.

Inspired by what is possible with the advent of big data technologies we invented The Customer Graph. We decided that by focusing on how similar customers consume products and services we can create a uniquely detailed and fluid picture of a brand's customers. The result of extensive research is the production of beautiful and insightful graphs that uncover the natural dynamic structure of customer communities. These uncover previously hidden opportunities for targeting with absolute clarity, specificity and timeliness. As a result marketers can dramatically improve their performance at each stage of the customer lifecycle.

We are working to make it easy to use in digital retail and travel businesses so we can revolutionise multi-channel customer centric marketing. If you’d like to know more contact us.
