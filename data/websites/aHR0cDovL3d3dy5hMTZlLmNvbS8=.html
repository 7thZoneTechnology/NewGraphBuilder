Append & Profile

Marketing to an anonymous customer is kind of like spitting into a hurricane. With today's technology and personalized messaging, generic marketing has become ineffective. 

Our Data Enhancement service turns your anonymous customers into well-defined segments and individuals. With over 2,000 demographic, behavioral, lifestyle and transactional variables available at the email or postal address level, you can give each customer the personal attention they deserve.

Data-Driven Personalization

Not all customers are created the same. Our sophisticated scoring platform helps you find your best customers based on real data.

Our 4D Data model integrates the most relevant data and our scoring engine makes these data actionable.

Customers use our real-time scoring platform in the following applications:

  • Score inbound leads to predict lead-to-customer conversion rates
  • Predict customer lifetime value at the time of first transaction
  • Find customers who are likely to leave your business before they do
