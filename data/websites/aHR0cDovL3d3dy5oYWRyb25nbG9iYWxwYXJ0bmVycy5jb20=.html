The term “Hadron” comes from the world of physics. It means particles held together by a strong force. As a Marketing Technology Data Holding Company we believe in the idea that the integration of proprietary technologies and marketing techniques will drive scale and efficiencies in unique ways. We focus on connecting dots through data analytics to unlock value by identifying opportunities and insights that drive growth and scale for our portfolio of companies and that of our Clients across many categories. We are a privately held company headquartered in Greenwich, Connecticut.
