  • Why idaciti

    Because we think a great way to get value
    from the world of big financial data
    is by using data to tell stories.


    Whether you are a financial analyst or an investor, a business academic or a data journalist, idaciti stories can promote your perspective or justify decisions,
    impress your peers or illuminate an issue that you care about.




  • IDACITI EXPLAINED

    idaciti is a social BI platform
    with a quick and simple workflow
    to let you create and share stories from data.

     

    Now investors, analysts, corporations and academics
    can connect and collaborate around
    global financial data.

     

    For our preview release, we are focusing on the high education market. We want to engage professors and students in a Linkedin like social environment to collaborate around the global financial data.

  • TELL YOUR STORY

     
    Get Data. Build Cards. Craft Story. Share Story.

    Data

    Leverage our curated data.
    Idaciti gives you access to a set of curated business and financial data to use as the foundation for your stories. We make it easy for you to find and connect data about companies and their performance.
     
     
     

    Card

    Visualize data on cards.
    An idaciti Card is used to visualize data to communicate information. We make it easy for you to display KPIs, charts, maps or other relevant content on your cards so that each card functions as an individual information insight.
     
     

    Story

    Connect cards into stories.
    An idaciti Story is a combination of cards connected into a compelling narrative. We make it easy for you to create a beginning, a middle and an end for your story and to share and publish your stories so they get the attention they deserve. Stories can be told with data, as well as with images, videos and words.

    Social

    Share stories to create collective intelligence.
    Idaciti encourages you to socialize your cards and stories. That's the best way to improve the quality of your information and benefit from the wisdom of the crowd or your trusted circle.
