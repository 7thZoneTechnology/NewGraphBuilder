Boomtown welcomes you to Boulder on November 12th, for our Fall 2014 Demo Day event at the Boulder Theater.

Each of the nine startup companies appearing at the event have been hand-picked by our selection committee to receive a $20,000 capital infusion and participate in our intensive 12-week accelerator program. Boomtown advisors and mentors have worked closely with all nine companies, helping them define their business models, refine their technologies and focus their marketing and fundraising activities. Don’t miss the opportunity to see these future stars at their launch event!

All ticket sales from Boomtown Demo Day go to  Entrepreneurship Fund of Colorado.

HOW IT WORKS:

Eight to ten companies are selected from the applicants to be a part of the program. Those companies are selected based on the strength of the team, the strength of the idea, and Boomtown’s ability to provide value with investment, mentoring and connections.

Boomtown was created for entrepreneurs, by entrepreneurs. Teams  get to spend 3 months developing their startups, surrounded by the resources they need most early on (branding and logo design, marketing, code feedback, usability testing). The resources and mentorship we provide are based on what we wished we’d had in past accelerator experiences.

$20K

 

Each startup receives:

  • $20k in investment
  • Office space in the heart of downtown Boulder
  • Design and marketing support from local and national firms
  • Mentoring from prominent engineers, entrepreneurs, investors, and thought leaders.
  • Over $60k in other benefits
  • Demo day at the end of the program bringing in top angels and VCs to see your company

GETTING YOUR STARTUP NOTICED

If you’ve got a technology-powered startup, you need to surround your idea with great engineers and entrepreneurs that have pulled their own companies off the ground. Check. We’ve got that. But if you’ve been at this for even a little while you’ve probably realized that the best idea isn’t going anywhere without baked-in design and marketing.

Boomtown takes full advantage of the unique nexus of technologists and design/marketing specialists that have fueled Boulder’s meteoric rise on the startup scene. Then we apply all that knowledge to help you build a product that people can’t stop using and can’t stop sharing.
