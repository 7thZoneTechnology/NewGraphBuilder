Background

Summary

Geniusly is a software-as-a-service company providing business intelligence and predictive analysis solutions for small and medium sized logistics companies in the US. Large enterprise solutions such as SAP and IBM are too robust and expensive for SME's and lighter solutions don't produce results without utilizing multiple different technologies together, requiring weeks of training and in the end nothing is tied in together.

Geniusly bridges that gap with a light-weight yet highly advanced TMS (Transportation Management System) constantly adjusting to our clients´ needs in one application. Users apply their own relevant information on top of the already robust layer of data customised to their needs, such as routes, currencies, commodities, date/time, taxes and so forth. Real-time tracking of assets is displayed live in the client´s browser or mobile device, adjusting real-time. A feedback loop helps it become better with each use.
