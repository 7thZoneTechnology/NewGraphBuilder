A ready-to-use technology that automatically gathers insights about customers, markets and companies, to identify your ideal clients.

A new way to improve your results.

Boost your knowledge of your clients with this ready-to-use big data platform. Kuldat helps small companies that have to find customers in new markets, as well as big enterprises that want to discover the unsatisfied needs that clients have in multiple markets. The technology has the following main features:
  • Analysis of customer needs
  • B2B prospecting
  • Industry and competitor monitoring.
  • The platform helps specific industries to discover clients' greatest unsatisfied needs related to products, prices, services and features.
  • Kuldat supports B2B companies in their prospecting activities, proposing a list of ideal leads.
  • The platform monitor competitors' marketing and sales activities such as store openings, new product launches, sales forecasts, etc.
