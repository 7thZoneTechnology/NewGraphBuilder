DNA.Bits

Cutting-edge technology that utilizes the Bitcoin platform to solve problems related to Big Data, HIPAA, and de-identified continuous sharing of genetic and correlated clinical data.

We apply Fin-Tech innovation (Bitcoin and Block-Chain platform) to solve the need for large sets of  clinical data and correlated  genetic samples. 

DNA.Bits enables:

  • Access to large sets of genetic data with the correlated clinical records.
  • Ongoing research to understand and enhance the effectiveness of current treatments.
  • Secure and anonymous insights into existing medical records.
  • Strong incentives for healthy people to contribute genetic data.
  • The ability to correlate clinical records with genetic data in a de-identified system. 
  • Convenient provision of the incentives or results to the owners of the records.
